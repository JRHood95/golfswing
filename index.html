Full Swing


By Julian Hood

Rythm, Sync, Rythm, Sync, Rythm, Sync! Nothing should feel rushed, focusing on club-path & club-face whilst keeping your eyes and head steady!!!

SET-UP & GRIP:


  1. 2.5 knuckles showing on the left hand, Right hand V pointing neutral straight up the grip, Both thumbs pinched in with your right thumb pistol gripping over the grip
  2. Stand tall to the ball, nice and comfortable with your back straight
  3. Create a solid stable base by tensing ur thighs and abs and pushing ur arse back
  4. Balance your weight in the middle of your feet
  5. Allow the arms to hang freely, nice and relaxed so that the butt of the club points at your belt buckle
  6. Get ur feet, knees, hips, forearms and shoulders parallel to the target

BACKSWING


START CALM & SMOOTH: With the hands staying passive, keep your right leg braced and your ass against the wall, so that your weight loads in to the inside of your right heel, as you swing the arms and club, leading with the right arm, whilst maintaining your triangle and allowing your left side to coil down through inertia


TRANSITION


SMOOTH & SYRUPY: Change direction and transition in to the downswing by, First, pushing your glutes in to a deeper position whilst the arms and club drop from the top, keeping your right heel planted


THROUGH-SWING


FIRE UP: Now u can rotate, as your weight shifts in to the left side, and swing the arms and club square through the ball, leading with the left arm, in to your balanced finish position
