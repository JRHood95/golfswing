Full Swing Thoughts



By Julian Hood

Rythm, Sync, Rythm, Sync, Rythm, Sync! Nothing should feel rushed, focusing on the clubs path & face from the start!!!

Focusing mainly on keeping your head position and posture, spine angle, over the ball throughout the entire swing till after impact, Don't force the turning of your body and come out of your posture!!!

SET-UP & GRIP:


  1. 2.5 knuckles showing on the left hand, Right hand V pointing neutral straight up the grip, Both thumbs pinched in with your right thumb pistol gripping over the grip
  2. Stand tall to the ball, nice and comfortable with your back straight
  3. Create a solid stable base by tensing ur thighs and abs and pushing ur arse back
  4. Balance your weight in the middle of your feet
  5. Allow the arms to hang freely, nice and relaxed so that the butt of the club points at your belt buckle
  6. Get ur feet, knees, hips, forearms and shoulders parallel to the target

BACKSWING


Keeping ur right leg stable, ur feet quiet and the arms and hands passive, Maintain ur triangle and turn the left side down, tracing the hands and club on a straight line back then allowing the club to set on plane as the right arm freely folds and takes everything in to position at the top


DOWNSWING


Smoothly transition in to the downswing. Unwinding in a way that drops the arms and club down in front of u through a solid impact whilst keeping ur head steady and the right heel down
